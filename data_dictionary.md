# Data Dictionary

| Column Name | Description | Data Type | Business Relevance |
|------------|-------------|-----------|-------------------|
| customer_id | Unique ID assigned to each customer | Integer | Helps identify individual customers |
| gender | Gender of the customer | Categorical | Used for demographic analysis |
| age | Age of the customer | Integer | Customer segmentation |
| country | Country of residence | Categorical | Region-wise sales analysis |
| signup_date | Date when customer registered | Date | Customer lifecycle tracking |
| last_purchase_date | Most recent purchase date | Date | Engagement analysis |
| total_purchase_amount | Total money spent by customer | Float | Revenue analysis |
